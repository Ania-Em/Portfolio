PROJECTS

BOHO DESIGN

Boho design studio is an interior design one-person business. The newly designed website had to clearly present the style of the designer to potential clients. The use of colours, typography and photos should reflect the characteristic boho style, yet still, keep it at the high level of elegance. The purpose of the website was also to advertise a new offer introduced that was furniture renovation and to accommodate the space for the designer to share the thoughts, ideas, tips and tricks for a Boho home.

DENTICO

Dentico is an application that allows patients of a private dental clinic to book doctor's appointments from anywhere and anytime. The access to all available slots of time of every dentist makes planning and fitting the visit into a busy schedule of a patient a lot easier. It improves the contact between clinic and patient, and also makes the whole process of dental care clearer and faster.

RUNNING APP

This project was created as an excercise during the recruitment process. The app is created for runners who want to monitor their training and progress. App is designed with flat and strong esthethics, providing the best functionalities for beginners but also semi-professional runners.
