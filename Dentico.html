ABOUT

The app is designed to be used by patients who visit the clinic regularly, and those who visit it only from time to time, mostly when they start having teeth pains or on a checkup appointment about once per year. The main function is to ease appointments booking - where the patient can choose the doctor, read about their qualifications and education, choose which type of treatment one needs and an hour and a day of the visit. All the booked appointments are visible in the calendar and can be changed or cancelled if needed. Dentico provides also information about the dental condition of the patient. It makes the patient aware of the exact dental problems one faces and being informed helps to take care of dental hygiene better and encourages to visit a dentist more often.

  13. Adjusting the design based on the conclusions from the tests.

User journey

structure

How to provide a user with enough information so they are certain about the doctor and the type of visit they are choosing?

  • Provide patients with a list of all the doctors working in a clinic - for each doctor it will be possible to see his education, accomplishments and work experience
  • There will be several types of most frequent types of appointments available, plus one categorised as "Planned treatment", it is available for patients who already have had an appointment with this choosen doctor and want to continue their treatment.

How to handle cancelling of the appointments? Till when is it possible?

  • The patient will have the opportunity to cancell their appointment up to 4 hours before said appointment. After this time the patient needs to call the clinic. Cancalation is accessible from appointment details screen.

How to make the whole process of going to the dentist less stressful?

  • The visit at the dentist can be very stressful, the medical procedure is always connected with some sort of uncomfort and anxiety. What the application can do is to minimise the stress before and after the visit.
  • Provide patient with full set of data, needed to get to and to be on time for the appointment - easily accessible contact details from the main menu- with map and navigation included, reminders set up individually for each visit so the patient will not miss it or rememeber about it at the last second.

How to help the patients take care of their dental hygiene more ?

  • Patients the insight into their's medical data at all times. They can see their ongoing medical issues, problems with specific teeth, so they are aware of state of their dental health.
  • Patients can set up recurring reminders that will remind them to go for a check-up visit to the dentist. On default this option is set up at every 6 months.

Style guide

How to make an application looking serious as for a medical related application and at the same time not too formal and boring?

  • Color people associate with elegance - blue
  • Color people associate with medicine - blueish green
  • Simplicity and transparence - sans-serif font
  • Modern touch - rounded edges and gradient

Usability tests

Using high-fidelity website's prototype I conducted usability tests on three individuals. My observations :

  • It took a long time for users to find the specific information in the app. They were confused and not certain about the contants of the four main categories on the home screen.
    > "My profile" was replaced by "My medical documentation" and "My calendar" by "My appointments".
  • After completing the booking process the user would be redirect immediately to the home screen, although I noticed that after that users would still open "My calendar" tab to see if their visit is in the system.
    > I decided to follow users' preferences and after completing the booking process redirect them to the calendar, where they can see the visit and they can also change the details of it.
  • Appointment's notifications were set up to: first notification: 1 day before the visit and another one 1 hour before the visit. Users were looking for a way to change it, as they didn't feel these dates were suitable for them.
    > The options to set up both notifications at any date and time was added.

Learnings

  • Users like to be given a choice - whateaver it's the time of the notifications or time of the dental appointment.
  • Every item that is on the home screen should be labelled with extra attention and should be tested, if some elements are labelled wrong here- at the beginning of the user's contact with an application it can lead to user's frustration, feeling lost and anxious and finally not using it anymore.
  • Sometimes what seems as a natural user flow might not be such, it is crucial to watch users perform simple tasks to watch what they are expecting to be next and what is natural flow for them when it comes to this specific application.
